The Language Technology and Society Research Group at the University of Groningen is dedicated to investigating the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and society, with an emphasis on natural language processing. Our research concentrates on developing computational methods for tasks related to argumentation, deliberation, and persuasion, with the goal of improving critical thinking and reducing bias in decision-making processes.

Collaboration

Interested in collaborating?

We welcome applications for externally funded positions at the PhD, internship, and postdoctoral levels. Some example funding opportunities include:

  • Australia: John Monash Scholarship
  • China: China Scholarship Council (CSC) with a top-up from the University of Groningen
  • Indonesia: LPDP/DIKTI Overseas Scholarship
  • Netherlands: NWO PhD in the Humanities (if you earned your Master's degree in the Netherlands)

You can also explore the grant finder for additional PhD opportunities in the Netherlands. We also support applications for the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Postdoctoral Fellowship.
